Removing the Barriers of Overseas Investing

At Icarus Wealth, we’re helping foreign investors access Indian market opportunities through a simple, secure, and low-cost app.

Who We Are

Founded in Wellington, New Zealand, we specialise in maximising ROI, building diverse portfolios, and creating strategies that generate passive income and long-term financial freedom.

Our Mission

To empower everyday investors by providing a secure, user-friendly platform that enables them to capitalise on the opportunities in India’s growing financial markets, while minimising costs and maximising growth potential.

Our Commitment

We are committed to transparency, security, and simplicity. Investing your hard-earned money should not feel overwhelming or risky. With Icarus Wealth, you’re backed by a platform that is transparent, simple, secure and designed to put the investor first.

Your Bridge to India’s Growing Market

We simplify the process and help you make confident investment decisions, no matter where you are. Here’s what you can expect when you invest abroad with us:

Seamless Onboarding

  • Simply sign up on our website or our app and browse through list of selected high performing mutual funds. Choose a fund and start investing.

Transparent Processes

  • No hidden costs or confusing jargon. Just clear, straightforward communication so you always know where your money is and how it’s performing.

Security and Compliance First

  • Your overseas investments are managed with strict safety standards, top-tier compliance measures, and trusted custodians to safeguard your capital.

ICARUS Wealth empowers individuals with smart investment solutions and financial education, helping you build a secure and sustainable future.
